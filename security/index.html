Security


FinanceAI takes security seriously and appreciates responsible disclosure of potential vulnerabilities.


If you believe you have found a security issue, please contact us at support@financeai.com and include:


  • A description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce
  • Affected URLs or endpoints
  • Any relevant screenshots or proof of concept
  • Your assessment of potential impact

Please do not access, modify, delete, or exfiltrate customer data, disrupt service availability, or perform testing that could affect other users.


Bug Bounty


FinanceAI does not currently operate a bug bounty program and does not offer monetary compensation for vulnerability reports.


We appreciate responsible disclosures and will review valid reports in good faith.
